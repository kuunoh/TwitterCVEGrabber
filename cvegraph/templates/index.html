Welcome

We are two students from UFR MIM University in Metz, France.
The purpose of this website is to provide you an analysis tool concerning the different CVEs.
You will be able to see the number of CVE occurrences discussed each day or week on Twitter and thus see what the potential trends are.
The graphs are updated every 6 hours.

This project is available as Open Source at the following address: https://github.com/kuunoh/TwitterCVEGrabber
Due to twitter rate limits, every update need to take place every 6 hours.
Right after updating, graphs can contains errors or not enough data due to that limit. To be sure to have consistent data, please wait few minutes (30 minutes to 1 hour after retrieving should be good) before analyse them.
